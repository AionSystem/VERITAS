🔒 What is the Sovereign Trace Protocol?
STP creates a permanent, tamper-evident record of any text, document, or decision. Each seal is:
• Bound to a SHA-256 cryptographic hash
• Stamped with Gregorian, Hebrew, and Dreamspell timestamps
• Stored in a public GitHub ledger
• Linked to a verifiable issue
This record cannot be altered or deleted. It is permanent.
⚠️ LEGAL NOTICE
This seal creates a permanent, immutable record in the Sovereign Trace public ledger.
Sealed content may be used as evidence in legal proceedings.
This record is permanent and cannot be deleted under any circumstances. Do not seal anything you may later need to remove.
Sealing privileged communications may waive legal privilege. Consult your legal counsel before sealing.
Do not seal classified, proprietary, or state-secret information. You assume all liability for the content you seal.
This action is irreversible. There is no deletion or modification after sealing.
📋 Data Retention & Access
• Seals are stored in public GitHub issues (permanently, globally accessible)
• Your IP address is not stored by VERITAS but may be logged by GitHub
• GitHub's Privacy Policy applies to issue data
• No encryption of issue content — assume public visibility
• Vercel (hosting) may have access to API requests as part of normal operations
• You may request deprecation of a seal (creates a new seal noting the deprecation), but the original remains

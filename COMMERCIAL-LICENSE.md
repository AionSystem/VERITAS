# VERITAS Commercial Licensing

## Who Needs a Commercial License?

- Companies embedding VERITAS in proprietary products
- Organizations using VERITAS in closed-source systems
- Any entity that cannot or will not comply with GPL terms

## Who Does NOT Need a Commercial License?

- Humanitarian organizations (UN, Red Cross, NGOs)
- Non-profits and educational institutions
- Individual researchers
- Government disaster agencies
- Any use that complies with GPL

CERTUS.AI

Confidence‑driven resource allocation simulator
🤔 How this simulator works:
• Load damage reports from VERITAS (JSON export or IndexedDB)
• Filter reports by DCI threshold and infrastructure type
• Sort by confidence (DCI × validity weight) or freshness
• Allocate available teams to highest-priority reports
• Results show recommended deployment order
Reports with SUSPENDED validity appear with red background and are deprioritized.
⚠️ DECISION-SUPPORT ONLY
This simulation assists human judgment — it does not replace it.
Do not use this simulation for life-critical decisions without human verification.
Final deployment decisions require field verification, especially for REVIEW REQUIRED reports.
All data processed locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server except when explicitly sealing a plan with STP.

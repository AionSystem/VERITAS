🔒 What is the Sovereign Trace Protocol?
STP creates a permanent, tamper-evident record of any text, document, or decision. Each seal is:
• Bound to a SHA-256 cryptographic hash
• Stamped with Gregorian, Hebrew, and Dreamspell timestamps
• Stored in a public GitHub ledger
• Linked to a verifiable issue
This record cannot be altered or deleted. It is permanent.
⚠️ LEGAL NOTICE
This seal creates a permanent, immutable record in the Sovereign Trace public ledger.
Sealed content may be used as evidence in legal proceedings.
Do not seal privileged, confidential, or legally protected information unless you have the authority to do so.
This action is irreversible. There is no deletion or modification after sealing.
